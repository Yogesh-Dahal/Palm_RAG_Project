INTERVIEW BOOKING GUIDELINES

To schedule an interview, please provide the following information:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Preferred Date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
- Preferred Time (HH:MM format)

Our interviews are conducted on weekdays from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Interview duration is typically 30-45 minutes.

Please note that all candidates must provide valid contact information.
Confirmation will be sent to the provided email address.
